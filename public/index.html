Hello and welcome to Tech by JR

Our goal is to provide the ability to understand simple Linux operating system command line information technology standards within a controlled emulated environment. After the modules provided are completed it will be easier to perform more advanced information technology tasks within stand alone applications such as a terminal emulator using Alpine linux within iSH Shell for iPhone or UserLand which allows for terminal emulation using a linux operating system of your choice on Android. There will be advanced concepts covered in future modules that will be published to the terminal emulator.
